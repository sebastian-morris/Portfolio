Pursa MRK3 Assembly

A 3D-printer assembly and calibration project focused on clean mechanical setup, tuning, and reliable output.

Overview

The assembly project treated the printer as a mechanical system where alignment, tension, calibration, and repeatability determine output quality. Each subsystem was built and validated before moving to the next.

Process

The work moved through frame construction, axis setup, belt tension, wiring, calibration, print testing, and small adjustments from observed defects. Nothing was assumed correct without a verified test print.

Gallery

Outcome

The printer assembly strengthened an understanding of how mechanical alignment and calibration work together in a practical tool. The machine produced consistent, reliable output from its first calibrated run.
