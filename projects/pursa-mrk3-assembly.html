Pursa MRK3 Assembly

A 3D-printer assembly and calibration project focused on clean mechanical setup, tuning, and reliable output.

Overview

The assembly project treated the printer as a mechanical system where alignment, tension, calibration, and repeatability determine output quality.

Process

The work moved through frame construction, axis setup, belt tension, wiring, calibration, print testing, and small adjustments from observed defects.

Outcome

The printer assembly strengthened an understanding of how mechanical alignment and calibration work together in a practical tool.
