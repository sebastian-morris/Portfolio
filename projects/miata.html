Miata

A lightweight sports-car project centered on maintenance, handling feel, and driver-focused refinement.

Overview

The Miata project uses a lightweight platform to explore how simple mechanical changes affect feel, reliability, and confidence.

Process

The work follows inspection, service planning, part evaluation, mechanical repair, road testing, and incremental refinement based on observed behavior.

Gallery

Outcome

The project developed a clearer sense for how small mechanical choices show up in steering, handling, and everyday reliability.
