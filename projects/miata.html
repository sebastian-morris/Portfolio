Miata

A lightweight sports-car project centered on maintenance, handling feel, and driver-focused refinement.

Overview

The Miata project uses a lightweight platform to explore how simple mechanical changes affect feel, reliability, and confidence. The car's accessible layout makes it ideal for hands-on learning without the complexity of heavier builds.

Process

The work follows inspection, service planning, part evaluation, mechanical repair, road testing, and incremental refinement based on observed behavior. Each step is documented before moving forward.
