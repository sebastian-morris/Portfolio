Sony A7iii Shutter Replacement

A precision repair project replacing a failed shutter assembly through careful disassembly and reassembly.

Overview

The shutter on my Sony A7iii failed during one of my commercial photoshoots. A repair quote placed the job at $400 to $600, and the camera was valued at about $800. At that price, the repair would have nearly totaled the camera. Instead of scrapping it, I decided to fix it myself.

Process

I sourced an OEM shutter replacement for $50. Once it arrived, I found online tutorials and documentation for the replacement process. As I disassembled the camera, I recorded the entire process in case I ran into issues during reassembly. I labeled screws and components as I went, trying to stay as organized as possible.

Gallery

Outcome

The camera came together perfectly and worked great. I used it for two more years on professional shoots before I sold it.
