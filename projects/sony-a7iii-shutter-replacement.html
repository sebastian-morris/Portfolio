Sony A7iii Shutter Replacement

A precision repair project replacing a failed shutter assembly through careful disassembly, handling, and testing.

Overview

This project replaced the shutter assembly in a Sony A7iii, combining photography knowledge with precision repair and careful bench organization.

Process

The process required staged disassembly, hardware tracking, ribbon-cable care, shutter replacement, reassembly, and verification through functional checks.

Gallery

Outcome

The repair reinforced the importance of clean work surfaces, controlled force, and methodical testing when working with compact electronics.
