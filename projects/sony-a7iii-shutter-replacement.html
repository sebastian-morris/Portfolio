Sony A7iii Shutter Replacement

A precision repair project replacing a failed shutter assembly through careful disassembly and reassembly.

Overview

The shutter on my Sony A7iii failed during one of my commercial photoshoots. Repair services charged $400 to $600 for a repair of this severity. The camera was valued at $800, so a $500 repair would have nearly totaled it. Rather than scrapping it, I decided to fix it myself.

Process

I sourced an OEM shutter replacement for $50. Once it arrived, I found online tutorials and documentation for the shutter replacement process. As I disassembled the camera, I recorded the entire process in case I ran into issues during reassembly. I labeled screws and components as I disassembled, trying to stay as organized as possible.

Gallery

Outcome

The camera came together perfectly and worked great. I used it for two more years on professional shoots before I sold it.
