Chevelle Restoration

A complete body-and-mechanical restoration of a 1971 Chevy Chevelle.

Overview

The Chevelle is a long-form personal project that combines classic-car teardown, repair planning, body prep, and finish work into a single sustained effort. Every decision is made to preserve the car's original character while maintaining a high standard of execution.

Process

Work has progressed through full teardown, panel assessment, surface preparation, hardware cataloging, and parts sourcing. Each phase requires careful judgment on repair versus replacement and consistent finish management across paint, chrome, and mechanical components.

Outcome
