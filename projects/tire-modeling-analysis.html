Tire Modeling Analysis

A vehicle dynamics study focused on tire behavior, suspension inputs, and model validation.

Overview

This analysis treats the tire as the critical link between setup decisions and measurable vehicle performance. The work connects theory, data, and testing into one practical engineering loop.

Process

The project involved reviewing data, comparing modeling assumptions, and translating tire behavior into setup questions for validation. It was built to support the way a Formula SAE team makes decisions on track.

Gallery

Outcome

The project strengthened the bridge between model-based thinking and real testing decisions. It sharpened how I interpret vehicle behavior and how I decide what to measure next.
